This is a project that visualized various variables of Lake Pontchartrain in Louisiana such as dissolved oxygen, salinity, pH, and fecal coliform levels, as pictured here. This was part of an annual initiative to visualize the health of the lake. The amount of sampling was limited, and as a result, kriging interpolation was used to predict levels that were outside of the sampling sites depicted by red dots.

This is a heat map depicting the concentration of electric scooter infractions that were committed during the pilot week of their introductions to the city of Knoxville, TN. Infractions were recorded on foot by taking pictures of them, as well as their GPS coordinates where they took place. The map was created using a google maps Javascript API. It was used by city officials in order to get a high level idea of where infractions were taking place in the city.

An alternative to the previous heat map. This one displays statistically significant areas of electric scooter activity across the city of Koxville, TN. It was made by using the optimized hot spot analysis tool from arcmap. The data came from the electric scooter company SPIN, which sent data of all the trips that were started in the city, and their corresponding coordinates. This map was used by city officials in order to determine where to put targeted safety outreach signs.
